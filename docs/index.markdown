### Biography

Shinyoung Kim is a Ph.D. student at the SoC Design Lab., Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Konkuk University, under the supervision of Prof. Chester Sungchung Park. He received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Konkuk University in 2023 and 2025, respectively. His research focuses on SoC design, virtual prototyping, and algorithm-hardware co-optimization for DNN accelerators.

### Research Interests

- Algorithm-Hardware Co-Optimization
- ASIP Design
